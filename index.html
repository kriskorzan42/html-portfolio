The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful story of hope and friendship. Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

Movie Details:

  • Director: Frank Darabont
  • Stars: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
  • Genre: Drama
  • Awards:
    • Academy Awards:
      • 7 Oscar nominations
      • Best Picture nomination
      • Best Actor nomination (Morgan Freeman)

The Godfather (1972)

The aging patriarch of an organized crime dynasty transfers control of his clandestine empire to his reluctant son.

Movie Details:

  • Director: Francis Ford Coppola
  • Stars: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino
  • Genre: Crime, Drama
  • Awards:
    • Academy Awards:
      • Won Best Picture
      • Won Best Actor (Marlon Brando)
      • Won Best Adapted Screenplay

Top 3 Memorable Scenes:

  1. The horse head scene
  2. The restaurant shooting
  3. The baptism montage

Pulp Fiction (1994)

The lives of two mob hitmen, a boxer, a gangster and his wife, and a pair of diner bandits intertwine in four tales of violence and redemption.

Movie Details:

  • Director: Quentin Tarantino
  • Stars: John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson
  • Genre: Crime, Drama
  • Awards:
    • Academy Awards:
      • Won Best Original Screenplay
      • Best Picture nomination
      • Best Actor nomination (John Travolta)
